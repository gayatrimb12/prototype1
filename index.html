AgeTech Connect: Older Adult Engagement Prototype

This prototype was created as a proposed improvement for AgeTech Connect. The goal is not to replace the company’s current work, but to support it by adding a more direct way for older adults to participate in the innovation process. Since AgeTech Connect already helps startups and innovators build solutions for aging populations, this prototype shows how the company could also involve older adults more directly through technology discovery, product testing, and user feedback.

Why this prototype was created

In my paper, I explain that one limitation of AgeTech Connect is that it mainly supports founders, investors, and professionals, while older adults are usually not the direct users of the platform itself. This prototype was created to show one possible improvement: a simple engagement portal where older adults can explore new technologies, try early concepts, and give feedback. This would help AgeTech Connect make its innovation process more user-centered and more closely connected to the real experiences of older adults.

Main idea: This prototype helps AgeTech Connect move from only supporting innovation behind the scenes to also including older adults in the process of shaping and improving those innovations.

Example technologies older adults could explore

Fall Detection Device

Health Support

Medication Reminder App

A simple reminder system with large text, voice prompts, and easy scheduling for everyday medication needs.

Social Connection

Video Check-In Tool

A simplified communication tool that helps older adults connect with family without complicated setup steps.
