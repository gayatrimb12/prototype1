AgeTech Connect: Older Adult Feedback Portal

This prototype shows how AgeTech Connect could improve its current model by giving older adults a direct role in exploring, testing, and reviewing technologies being developed for them. Instead of only supporting startups behind the scenes, this version makes older adults active participants.

Why this improvement matters

AgeTech Connect already does a strong job of building innovation and connecting startups, investors, and healthcare partners. However, one limitation is that older adults are often not directly involved in the experience. This portal closes that gap by creating a simple space where older adults can explore tools, try early concepts, and share feedback that helps improve real products.

What AgeTech Connect learns

  • Ease of use: Are directions simple enough for older adults?
  • Accessibility: Does the product use readable text and clear navigation?
  • Value: Does the tool actually solve a real problem?
  • Trust: Would users feel comfortable using it at home?
How this is different from Waterlily: This prototype is not about long-term care forecasting or financial prediction. It is specifically focused on helping AgeTech Connect become more user-centered by allowing older adults to discover and shape innovation directly.
